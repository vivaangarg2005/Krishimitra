MGNREGA (Rural Employment Guarantee)

Eligibility: Rural households willing to do unskilled manual work.

Benefit: 100 days of paid work every year through local public projects.

Apply via: Local Gram Panchayat (Job card issued post-verification).

PMFBY (Crop Insurance)

Eligibility: Farmers growing notified crops.

Benefit: Insurance cover for crop loss due to natural disasters.

Apply via: Enroll through local agriculture office or banks.
