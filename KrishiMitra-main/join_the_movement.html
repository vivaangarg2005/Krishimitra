👩‍🌾 Women Empowerment Cell

Welcome to the Women Empowerment Cell. We’re here to support women in rural communities with access to jobs, skill training, support groups, and financial independence tools.

🎓 Digital Literacy Training

Learn basic smartphone skills, WhatsApp use, online forms, government apps, and job search platforms.

💼 Job Matching Support

Access to curated job listings for women in tailoring, teaching, handicrafts, and agri-business support roles.

👭 Community Support Groups

Join safe local groups to discuss work, family, health, and financial planning — led by trained facilitators.

💰 Microfinance Guidance

Learn how to open bank accounts, get SHG loans, and manage income to become self-reliant.

🧵 Skill Development Workshops

Regular sessions in stitching, food processing, beauty services, and home-based entrepreneurship.

📱 Mobile App Training

Hands-on training to use useful apps — telemedicine, crop selling, banking, and women’s helplines.
